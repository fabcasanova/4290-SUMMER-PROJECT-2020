Directors

Dr. Yuanjie He

Is a Professor and Chair of Technology and Operations Management Department in CBA Cal Poly Pomona. He received his Ph.D. in Operations from Weatherhead School of Management, Case Western Reserve University in 2005. His main research and teaching interests are in the areas of supply chain management and business analytics with over twenty high quality publications on contemporary topics such as sharing economy, sustainability, and supply chain risk management etc. He has also been leading the College of Business Administration’s efforts in developing master level programs including the Master of Science in Business Analytics (MSBA) and the Master of Science in Digital Supply Chain Management (MSDSCM).

Dr. Drew Hwang

Is a Professor and the Chair of the Computer Information Systems department at Cal Poly Pomona and a visiting professor of the Global Business Graduate Program at the Soochow University, Taiwan. He received a Ph.D. degree in Management Information Systems in 1995 from University of Mississippi. Dr. Hwang has had more than ten years of industry experiences as a system analyst, project manager, and chief technology officer in the areas of e-commerce and business information systems. He was the founder director for the Mitchell C. Hill Center for Applied Business Technology (MCHABIT) of the College of Business at Cal Poly Pomona in 2017 and the chief developer of eHongKong.com in 2001.
